## Notes for GPT

When scouting, start with the sources listed above. These are the analyst's edge.

Companies found through these source paths are more likely to be:
- Unknown to mainstream analysts
- Earlier in their development
- Less competed for in deal flow
- More aligned with authentic fund sourcing

Always record the exact source URL in the `source_url` field so the analyst can verify and follow the trail.
