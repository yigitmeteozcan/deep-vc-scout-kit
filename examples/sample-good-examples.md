## Example 1

**Company:**
ShiftLog OY (fictional)

**Why it is a good fit:**
Pre-seed industrial SaaS company replacing paper-based maintenance logs at Baltic manufacturing plants. Founder spent 7 years as a maintenance engineer at a pulp mill before building the product. First customer is a paying manufacturer in Finland. Stage, sector, buyer type, and geography all match fund thesis precisely.

**Source path:**
Found on the Aalto University technology transfer office portfolio page. Not yet in Crunchbase or any Finnish startup media.

**Signals:**
- Founder-market fit: 7 years as maintenance engineer before founding
- Specific workflow replacement: paper maintenance logs → mobile app
- Paying customer confirmed: named Finnish manufacturer
- University spinout with no prior media coverage
- Technical co-founder with embedded systems background

**Pattern to find more like this:**
Search university technology transfer offices in Estonia, Finland, and Lithuania. Look for founders with 5+ years of operational experience in a specific industrial sector before building. Prioritize companies that replace a physical or paper-based workflow, not a digital one.
